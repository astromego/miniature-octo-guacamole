A collection of icon designs inspired by the constellations of the zodiac.


Two sets of 12 designs, available as digital downloads, stickers and enamel pins!



Sun Signs; The twelve constellations of the zodiac stripped back to their most elegant, in a white and gold colourway. Inspired by the solar aspect of your birth chart. Available as digital downloads and stickers coming soon! Top row, L-R; Aries, Taurus, Gemini, Cancer, Leo and Virgo. Bottom row, L-R; Libra, Scorpio, Sagittarius, Capricorn, Aquarius and Pisces.



Moon Signs; The twelve constellations nestled against a backdrop of stars, held in the embrace of a crescent moon. These reflect the lunar aspect of one's birth chart. Available as digital downloads and stickers. Iron on patches and enamel pins will be available soon.
